Common Questions

How do I sign in?

Choose your Mastodon instance, continue to its login page, and authorize fediverse.video. We never see your password.

How do I switch accounts?

Use the account switcher in the app to add or select another Mastodon account. Tokens are stored securely on your device.

Why can’t I see certain posts?

Content visibility depends on your chosen Mastodon instance and its moderation rules. Check the instance’s policies if posts appear limited.

How do I report a bug?

Send steps to reproduce, device model, OS version, and screenshots if possible to fediverse.video@discover-artists.com.

Troubleshooting

  • App won’t load: Check network connectivity and try a pull-to-refresh.
  • Media won’t upload: Ensure camera/photo permissions are granted; try smaller files.
  • Auth issues: Sign out and sign back in; confirm your Mastodon instance is reachable.
