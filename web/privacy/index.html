Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-07-27 Last Updated: 2025-07-27

Your privacy is sacred to us.

Information We Collect

Authentication Information

  • OAuth Tokens: Only the tokens needed to connect to your chosen Mastodon instance.
  • Instance Information: The URL of the Mastodon instance you choose.
  • We do not store usernames, passwords, or other personal credentials on our servers.

Local Data Storage

  • Feed Cache: Cached locally on your device (IndexedDB on web, local storage on iOS) to improve performance.
  • User Preferences: Stored locally on your device.
  • All cached data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information necessary for the app to function:

  • Device type and operating system version (iOS app only)
  • App version information
  • Basic usage analytics to improve performance (crash reports, performance metrics)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal browsing activity or behavior tracking
  • Marketing data or advertising identifiers
  • Social media content beyond what is needed for display
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or device information beyond what is required for app functionality
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond OAuth tokens

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you with your chosen Mastodon instance
  • Display feeds and content from that instance
  • Cache data locally to improve performance
  • Provide technical support when requested
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

  • Mastodon Instances: We communicate only with the Mastodon instance you choose, sharing only your authentication token to retrieve feeds.
  • masto.js: We use the open-source masto.js library to communicate with Mastodon instances.

No Marketing or Advertising Partners

We do not share your information with marketing companies, advertisers, or data brokers.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, but only to the minimum extent necessary.

Data Security

  • OAuth tokens are securely stored and transmitted.
  • All communications with Mastodon instances use HTTPS.
  • Local data storage follows platform security best practices.
  • We regularly update security practices and dependencies.

Data Retention

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored until you disconnect your account or uninstall the app.
  • Cached Feed Data: Managed by your device storage; can be cleared at any time.
  • Technical Data: Retained only as long as necessary for app functionality and improvement.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Disconnect your Mastodon account at any time.
  • Clear cached data through app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • Contact us with questions about your data.

Data Deletion

  1. Disconnect your account in the app settings.
  2. Clear the app's cache/data through device settings.
  3. Uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware of such data, contact us so we can delete it.

International Users

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting the new policy here, updating the \"Last Updated\" date, and providing in-app notifications for significant changes.

Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to other services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third parties. Please review their policies separately.

Open Source Commitment

fediverse.video is committed to transparency. Our use of the open-source masto.js library ensures interactions with Mastodon instances follow established, auditable protocols.
